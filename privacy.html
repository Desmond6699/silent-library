Privacy Policy

Mock-up privacy content for the Silent Library prototype website.

1. Introduction

This page explains how visitor information may be handled on the Silent Library website prototype. This is sample content for assignment purposes.

2. Information Handling

The prototype does not collect, store, or transmit real personal data. Any forms shown on the website are mock-up forms for visual demonstration only.

3. Mock Forms and Data Use

The contact form and borrow book interface are included to show how the website may work in a future system. They do not connect to a backend or database.

4. Cookies and Website Notice

This prototype does not use cookies for tracking. Future production versions should include a clearer cookie and privacy notice if tracking or analytics are added.

5. Contact and Updates

For privacy questions, visitors may contact the library using the contact page. This policy may be updated if the prototype becomes a real working website.
